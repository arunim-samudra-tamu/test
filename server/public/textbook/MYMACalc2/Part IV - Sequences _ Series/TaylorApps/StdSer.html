Table of Standard Maclaurin Series

\[\begin{aligned} e^x&=\sum_{n=0}^\infty \dfrac{x^n}{n!}& \dfrac{1}{1-x}&=\sum_{n=0}^\infty x^n \\ &=1+x+\dfrac{x^2}{2}+\dfrac{x^3}{3!}+\cdots\quad& &=1+x+x^2+x^3+\cdots \\ \sin x&=\sum_{k=0}^\infty (-1)^k\dfrac{x^{2k+1}}{(2k+1)!}& \ln(1+x)&=\sum_{n=1}^\infty \dfrac{(-1)^{n-1}}{n}x^n \\ &=x-\dfrac{x^3}{3!}+\dfrac{x^5}{5!}-\cdots& &=x-\dfrac{x^2}{2}+\dfrac{x^3}{3}-\cdots \\ \cos x&=\sum_{k=0}^\infty (-1)^k\dfrac{x^{2k}}{(2k)!}& \arctan x&=\sum_{n=0}^\infty \dfrac{(-1)^n}{2n+1}x^{2n+1} \\ &=1-\dfrac{x^2}{2!}+\dfrac{x^4}{4!}-\cdots& &=x-\dfrac{x^3}{3}+\dfrac{x^5}{5}-\cdots \end{aligned}\]
