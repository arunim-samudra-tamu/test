Tip the Die

Winner:
  • Click on to start a new game with a random face as the new starting face.

  • Click the green buttons to tip the die so that the associated face is on top. You can tip the die as many times as you want. The blue arrow indicates the previous top face. Click on to return the blue arrow and the associated face to the top.

  • Click on to end the turn, but you can only end your turn on a face one tip away from the previous top face.

  • When you end your turn, the value of the new top face is added to the current total score, and it becomes the other player's turn.

  • Play continues until one player either reaches 31 and wins, or pushes the score higher than 31 and loses.
