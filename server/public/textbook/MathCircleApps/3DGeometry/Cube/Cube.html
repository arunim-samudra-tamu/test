Cube Symmetries

Cube Symmetries

  • Change the color of the verticies by clicking on them. The color will cycle between black, blue and red.

  • Click on the colored shapes to rotate the cube around the associated axis. Blue shapes will rotate the cube counter clockwise, while pink shapes will rotate it clockwise.

  • The different shapes are associated with different types of axes. A square will rotate around the center of a face, a line will rotate around the center of an edge, or a triangle will rotate around a diagonal through vertices.
